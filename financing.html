Flexible Financing

Owning your dream vehicle has never been easier. We offer tailored financial packages, direct import financing, and competitive trade-in valuations.

Import Finance

Looking to import a specific unit directly from Japan or the UK? We handle the logistics, customs, and duty payments. Secure your unit with a 30% deposit, and we will finance the rest upon arrival.

  • Up to 70% duty financing
  • Seamless port-to-door delivery
  • Competitive local interest rates

Local Asset Finance

For units currently sitting in our showroom floor, we partner with top tier banks to provide fast-tracked asset financing. Drive away within 48 hours of approval.

  • Repayment periods of up to 60 months
  • Quick appraisal and approval process
  • Trade-ins accepted as down payments
